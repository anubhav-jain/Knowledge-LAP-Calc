Knowledge LAP provisioning calculator

This is a tool to calculate what limits are pre-approved for knowledge provisioning. Enter the new limits that the customer has requested in the boxes below. If you see 4 green results (3 for the limits you enter and 1 cumulative at the bottom), then you are cleared to make the change without any additional clearance. If you see one or more red boxes, then please go ahead and file a LAP here : Link
